About Puodho

A rural farm built on freshness and trust.

Puodho began with a simple promise: make fresh farm produce easier for nearby homes and small businesses to reserve directly from the source.

Today, Puodho focuses on broiler chickens and eggs, with room to grow into goats, sheep, cows, and other produce as local demand grows sustainably.

Every order is handled personally so customers know what is available, when it can be picked up, and whether delivery is practical.
