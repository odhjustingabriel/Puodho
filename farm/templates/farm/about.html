About Us

A rural farm built on freshness, trust, and direct connection.

Puodho serves nearby homes and small businesses with poultry, eggs, livestock, and milk products that are simple to request and personally confirmed.

Puodho began with a simple promise: make fresh farm produce easier for nearby homes and small businesses to order directly from the source.

Today, Puodho lists broiler chickens, eggs, cattle, goats, sheep, and milk products, while still treating every request as subject to real farm availability, animal age, breed suitability, and sustainable demand.

Every order or livestock inquiry is handled personally so customers know what is available, what option or breed fits their need, when it can be picked up, and whether delivery is practical.

What guides us

  • Fresh produce and healthy livestock handled with care.
  • Clear communication before every order or inquiry is fulfilled.
  • Fair, direct relationships with local customers.
  • Steady growth that respects farm capacity and demand.

Our Mission

To provide fresh poultry, eggs, livestock, and milk products through direct requests, clear confirmation, and dependable service for nearby households, farms, and businesses.

Our Vision

To grow Puodho into a respected local farm brand known for quality produce, healthy animals, honest customer relationships, and sustainable rural enterprise.
