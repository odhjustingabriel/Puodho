About Us

A rural farm built on freshness, trust, and direct connection.

Puodho serves nearby homes and small businesses with farm produce that is simple to reserve and personally confirmed.

Puodho began with a simple promise: make fresh farm produce easier for nearby homes and small businesses to order directly from the source.

Today, Puodho focuses on broiler chickens and eggs, with room to grow into goats, sheep, cows, and other produce as local demand grows sustainably.

Every order is handled personally so customers know what is available, when it can be picked up, and whether delivery is practical.

What guides us

  • Fresh produce handled with care.
  • Clear communication before every order is fulfilled.
  • Fair, direct relationships with local customers.
  • Steady growth that respects farm capacity and demand.

Our Mission

To provide fresh, trustworthy farm produce through direct ordering, clear confirmation, and dependable service for nearby households and businesses.

Our Vision

To grow Puodho into a respected local farm brand known for quality produce, honest customer relationships, and sustainable rural enterprise.
